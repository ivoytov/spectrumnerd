About Spectrum Nerd

This tool is for every analyst who has tried to get a clear picture of how much spectrum each wireless carrier owns, and where. FCC tools are slow and don't include population statistics. Auction results are not integrated into anything. And because FCC changes its geographic definitions at every other auction, it's very hard to get a sense of what areas are actually covered by how much spectrum. I created this tool after being frustrated digging through FCC's hodge podge of websites, APIs, auction records and making a myriad of spreadsheets and Qlik dashboards to tie them together.

This database is compiled from FCC's public spectrum API and combined with auction results from FCC's administered auctions. Spectrum is frequently traded amongst carriers and other players - this tool does not include private transaction data. Mobile Satellite Services (MSS) spectrum is not licensed by the FCC and is therefore not included in this tool. That mainly affects Inmarsat and LightSquared.
