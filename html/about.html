About Spectrum Nerd

My name is Ilya Voytov and I created this tool after being frustrated digging through FCC wireless spectrum licenses. Use this tool to see how much population a carrier's spectrum portfolio covers. If there are overlapping licenses covering the same region, this tool will avoid double counting the population that lives in that region. For example, look up Ntelos and click on the 1,870 MHz band - there are 3 different spectrum licenses listed, all with different call signs but 2 of them actually cover the exact same population living in Richmond, VA (MTA023 market area). Where available, I also included price paid data from past spectrum auctions and calculated the price per population covered by 1 MHz (number of MHz times population covered by the license or $/MHz-POP).
This database is compiled from FCC's public spectrum API and combined with auction results from FCC's administered auctions.
